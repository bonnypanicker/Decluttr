Decluttr Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Decluttr helps you archive app context before uninstalling apps. We process account, archive, and diagnostics data to operate this feature safely.

What We Collect

Email (if signed in), archive metadata (package ID, app name, notes, tags, folder names, archived timestamp), and diagnostics logs for reliability.

Why We Collect It

To sync your archive across devices, show archive history, and improve reliability.

Billing Data

Premium verification uses Google Play Billing and server-side token verification. We store entitlement metadata, not raw payment card details.

Your Controls

You can sign out, delete archived items, export/import archive data, and revoke permissions in Android Settings.
