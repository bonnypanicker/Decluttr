Decluttr Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This page explains how Decluttr users can request account deletion and what happens to associated data.

How to Request Deletion

  • Email us at bonnyregipanicker@proton.me from your registered email address.
  • Use subject: Decluttr Account Deletion Request.
  • Include your Firebase account email used in Decluttr.

We will confirm your request and start processing deletion.

What Data Is Deleted

  • Decluttr account access associated with your Firebase Authentication user.
  • Cloud archive records stored under your user path in Firestore.
  • Folder names, notes, tags, and metadata stored for your cloud archive.
  • Cloud entitlement profile linked to your user document (where applicable).

What May Be Retained

  • Security, fraud-prevention, and legal-compliance records as required by law.
  • Financial transaction records required for audit, tax, or dispute handling.
  • Aggregated or de-identified analytics that cannot identify you directly.

Typical retention for limited legal/compliance records is up to 180 days unless longer retention is required by law.

Processing Time

We target completion within 7 business days, and no later than 30 days from verification of your request.

Important Notes

  • Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Deleting local app data on your device can be done anytime from Android app settings.
  • For policy details, see our Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions.
